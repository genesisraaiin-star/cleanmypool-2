CleanMyPool LLC — Pool Service Agreement

This Pool Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between CleanMyPool LLC ("Company") and the client identified above ("Client"). By signing below, Client agrees to all terms stated herein.

1. Services Provided

Company will perform weekly pool cleaning services at the service address provided. Each visit includes: water testing and chemical balancing, brushing pool walls and floor, netting and skimming debris, emptying pump and skimmer baskets, and visual inspection of pool equipment. Monthly services include filter cleaning (cartridge rinse or DE backwash as applicable).

2. Service Schedule

Service is performed once per week on a consistent assigned day. Client will be notified of their service day prior to first visit. Company will provide advance notice for any schedule changes. Client does not need to be present during service.

3. Pricing & Billing

Client agrees to the monthly flat rate selected above. Billing occurs at the beginning of each service month. A valid credit card must be kept on file for automatic billing. Returned payments are subject to a $25 returned payment fee. Pricing is subject to change with 30 days written notice.

4. Month-to-Month / No Contract

This Agreement is month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Either party may cancel with reasonable notice. No cancellation fee is charged. Client may cancel by phone, text, or email at any time.

5. Access & Site Conditions

Client grants Company and its contractors reasonable access to the pool area on the scheduled service day. Client is responsible for ensuring safe, unobstructed access. Client must disclose any known hazards including aggressive pets, unmarked steps, or unstable surfaces. Company is not liable for damage caused by pre-existing conditions or lack of disclosed access issues.

6. Minor Repairs

Company technicians may perform minor repairs (under $75 value) without prior authorization to maintain pool safety and equipment function. Repairs exceeding this threshold require Client approval before work is performed. Client will be notified of any recommended repairs observed during service visits.

7. Chemical Responsibility

Company provides all pool chemicals as part of the monthly service rate. Company is not responsible for algae blooms, water problems, or chemical imbalances caused by external contamination (heavy storms, bather load, wildlife, etc.) between scheduled visits. Additional service calls are available at an hourly rate.

8. Limitation of Liability

Company's liability is limited to the cost of services rendered in the current month. Company is not liable for pre-existing equipment damage, acts of nature, or circumstances beyond its reasonable control. Company carries general liability insurance; certificate available upon request.

9. Disputes

Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be resolved by good-faith negotiation. Governing law: State of Florida. Venue: Hillsborough County.

10. Agreement Acceptance

By signing below, Client acknowledges they have read, understood, and agree to the terms of this Agreement. Electronic signatures are legally binding under the ESIGN Act and Florida Electronic Signature Act.
