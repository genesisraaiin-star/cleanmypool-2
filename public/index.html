FAQ

Common
questions.

Is there a contract?
+

No contracts — ever. CleanMyPool is month-to-month. Cancel any time before the month ends with zero penalties or fees.

How much does pool cleaning cost in Tampa?
+

Flat monthly rates starting at $165/mo for screened pools with a salt system, $185/mo for open pools with salt, and $205/mo for screened pools with chlorine. No hidden fees, ever.

How does billing work?
+

We charge your card on file a few days before the start of each month. Because we're contract-free, payment is required upfront each month. Cancel before month's end and you're all set — no mid-month refunds.

Do I need to be home for service?
+

No — most customers aren't home. We service the same day every week so you always know when to expect us.

What areas do you serve?
+

We serve South Tampa including Hyde Park, Palma Ceia, Davis Islands, Bayshore, and Ballast Point — plus Westchase, Carrollwood, Lutz, Wesley Chapel, and surrounding Tampa Bay neighborhoods. Not sure if we cover your area? Call us.

Do you service salt water and chlorine pools?
+

Yes. We service both salt water and traditional chlorine pools across Tampa Bay. Our flat monthly pricing reflects the type of system your pool uses.

Are you a licensed pool contractor?
+

Yes. CleanMyPool LLC holds Florida pool contractor license CPC1460147 and is an authorized San Juan Pools fiberglass dealer and installer. You're in good hands.

What if my pool needs a repair?
+

Our sister company FixMyPool.pro handles all repairs — equipment, plumbing, renovation, resurfacing, and new builds. We'll coordinate everything seamlessly for you.

How often will my pool be cleaned?
+

Every week, same day, same technician. Weekly service is essential in Florida's climate to keep algae and chemical levels in check year-round — especially during Tampa's hot, rainy summers.

How do I get started?
+

Sign up online in under 60 seconds — pick your plan, enter your address, add a card on file. We confirm your first service date within 1 business day. The fastest pool service signup in Florida.
